    About Our Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT) Surgery Services in Chicago

    Our ENT specialists in Chicago provide complete care options for adults and children with ear, nose, throat, and head and neck conditions. With a focus on making your experience as seamless and convenient as possible, our department strives to provide prompt, compassionate care and state-of-the-art surgical techniques.

    ENT physicians at Rush collaborate with experts in audiology, sleep-language pathology, neurosurgery, radiation oncology, allergies and pulmonology, along with other specialists. Together, they address the full scope of problems affecting the ears, nose, throat, and head and neck, including hearing loss, sinusitis and sleep apnea. Theyve been designated as a Multidisciplinary Team of Distinction by the North American Skull Base Society.

    This team provides personalized treatment plans in what are often complex cases and is dedicated to bettering patient care through education and research both in the lab and in the clinic.

    U.S. News & World Report includes Rush University Medical Center on its Best Hospitals Honor Roll and ranks our ear, nose and throat program among the nations best, as well as our neurology and neurosurgery programs. Chicago magazine names many of our ENT doctors on its list of Top Docs in Cook County, and Castle Connolly includes several Rush specialists on its list of Top Doctors in Chicago.

    FAQs About Rush Otolaryngology (ENT) in Chicago

    To schedule an appointment with our ENT providers, call (888) 352-7874, and a care coordinator will help you. You can also visit our Make your appointment page to find online scheduling options and learn more.

    If you are currently a patient at Rush, you can schedule an appointment via MyChart.

    And if you know the name of the physician youd like to see, you can scroll down to the Our Team of ENT Doctors and Providers section on this page, click on the providers profile and use the Make an Appointment feature on their page.

    Parking

    You can park in the main parking garage at Rush University Medical Center on the southeast corner of Paulina and Harrison streets. It is enclosed and has a covered walkway that connects to the Sofija and Jorge O. Galante Orthopedic Building, where you can find ENT services in Suite 550.

    Valet parking is also available at the building, 1611 W. Harrison St., Monday through Friday from 6:30 a.m. to 6 p.m.

    For parking rates and more information, visit our Parking page.

    Public transportation

    There are several CTA public transportation options that stop at or near the building. They include the following:

    • Blue Line L Illinois Medical District stop
    • Pink Line L Polk stop
    • #7 Harrison Bus (weekdays) Harrison and Ashland stop
    • #9 Ashland Bus (weekdays and weekends) Ashland and Harrison stop
    • #126 Jackson Bus (weekdays and weekends) Jackson and Ashland stop
    • #157 Streeterville/Taylor Bus (weekdays) Polk Pink Line Station stop
    • PACE Route 755 (weekdays, rush-hour) Harrison and Paulina stop

    For specific directions from each stop to the building and more information visit our Public Transportation page.

    You can contact us during our normal business hours. To schedule an appointment, call (888) 352-7874. To reach our office, call (312) 942-6100. You can also send us a fax at (312) 942-6225.

    The best way to find out which Rush locations, services and physicians are covered by your plan is to reach out to your insurance provider directly. But you may be able to find some general answers to your questions on our Insurance page.
